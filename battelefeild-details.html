The Battlefeild

Project information

  • Concept Treatment: battlefield_concept_treatment.pdf
  • Rules: battlefeild_rules.pdf
  • Playtest Report: battlefeild_playtest_report.pdf

A thrilling player vs. player card game where strategy and resource management are key. Two players face off with a deck of 15 cards each, consisting of close combat units, ranged combat units, heavy combat units, special characters, and wild cards. The game unfolds on a battlefield of three lanes per player, where each card has unique values and special abilities.

Highlights:

  • Gameplay: Players draw 13 random cards and select 2 of their choice, with opportunities to draw additional cards in later rounds.
  • Management: Balancing resource usage is crucial, as players must decide when to push forward or hold back for future rounds.
  • Target Audience: Suitable for players aged 8 and above, offering fun for families with a round-robin system.
  • Player Experience: A balanced mix of randomness and skill, where strategic decision-making and resource management create a dynamic experience.
  • Dramatic Elements: Command armies, manage resources, and engage in tense battles filled with conflict and drama, especially with the impactful wild cards.
  • Objective: Win by outscoring your opponent in two out of three rounds through clever card play and strategic maneuvers.
